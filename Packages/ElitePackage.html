Package Overview

Indulge in the ultimate 14-day luxury travel experience. The Voyager Package is crafted for discerning travelers who expect nothing but the finest — from business class flights and 5-star resort stays to private daily tours and a dedicated personal guide. Every detail is handled so you can focus on enjoying the journey.

Itinerary

Day 1 — Arrival in Nusa Dua

Arrival at Ngurah Rai International Airport, transfer to 5-star resort (The Mulia or Grand Hyatt Bali). Check-in and welcome dinner by the beach.

Day 2 — Kuta & Seminyak

Explore Kuta Beach and Poppies Lane, then visit Seminyak boutiques. Sunset at Double Six Beach and dinner at Merah Putih.

Day 3 — Uluwatu & Kecak Dance

Visit Uluwatu Temple, enjoy cliff views, watch Kecak Fire Dance at sunset, then dinner at Jimbaran Bay.

Day 4 — Tanah Lot & Canggu

Explore Tanah Lot Temple, then visit Canggu’s Echo Beach and cafés. Optional surf activity.

Day 5 — Nusa Penida Tour

Full-day island tour: Kelingking Beach, Crystal Bay, Broken Beach, and Angel’s Billabong.

Day 6 — Spa & Beach Day

Relax with a Balinese spa treatment and enjoy free time at Nusa Dua Beach.

Day 7 — Ubud & Rice Terraces

Visit Tegallalang Rice Terraces, optional Bali Swing, then explore Ubud Palace and Art Market.

Day 8 — Monkey Forest & Cooking Class

Visit Sacred Monkey Forest and join a Balinese cooking class with local dishes.

Day 9 — Mount Batur Sunrise Trek

Early hike to Mount Batur for sunrise views, followed by hot spring relaxation.

Day 10 — Tirta Empul & Kintamani

Visit Tirta Empul Temple, then enjoy lunch with views of Lake Batur and coffee tasting.

Day 11 — Lempuyang & East Bali

Visit Lempuyang Temple (Gateway to Heaven) and relax at Virgin Beach in Candidasa.

Day 12 — Besakih & Klungkung

Explore Besakih Temple and visit Kerta Gosa and Klungkung Market.

Day 13 — Free Day & Farewell Dinner

Free day at leisure with optional spa. Farewell dinner at the resort or Locavore Ubud.

Day 14 — Departure

Breakfast, last-minute shopping, then transfer to Ngurah Rai International Airport.
