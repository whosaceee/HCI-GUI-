Package Overview

Get the full Bali experience in this 8-day balanced package — the perfect middle ground between budget and luxury. Explore iconic destinations like Uluwatu, Tanah Lot, Ubud, and Tegallalang. With private transfers, a licensed guide, and a 4-star hotel, this package ensures a smooth and memorable trip.

Itinerary

Day 1 — Arrival

Arrive at Ngurah Rai Airport. Transfer to your 4-star hotel in Seminyak or Ubud. Enjoy a sunset walk at Seminyak Beach and dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 2 — Kuta & Canggu

Visit Kuta Beach for swimming or surfing. Explore Poppies Lane, then head to Canggu for cafés, boutiques, and sunset at Echo Beach.

Day 3 — Uluwatu & Kecak Dance

Visit Uluwatu Temple on a cliffside. Watch the Kecak Fire Dance at sunset, then enjoy seafood dinner at Jimbaran Bay.

Day 4 — Tanah Lot & Seminyak

Explore Tanah Lot Temple and enjoy its famous sunset view. Return to Seminyak for shopping and dinner.

Day 5 — Ubud Tour

Visit Monkey Forest, Ubud Royal Palace, and Art Market. Enjoy local food and explore Bali's cultural center.

Day 6 — Rice Terraces

Visit Tegallalang Rice Terraces. Optional Bali Swing and coffee tasting at a local plantation.

Day 7 — Temples & Volcano

Visit Tirta Empul Temple, then head to Kintamani for views of Mount Batur and Lake Batur. Stop by a traditional village.

Day 8 — Departure

Free time for shopping before transfer to the airport for departure.
