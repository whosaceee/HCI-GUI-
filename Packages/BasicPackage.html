Package Overview

Experience the magic of Bali in this 5-day introductory package, perfect for first-time visitors and budget-conscious travelers. You will visit iconic temples, walk through the lively streets of Kuta, watch a legendary sunset at Tanah Lot, and get a taste of authentic Balinese culture — all with comfortable accommodation and hassle-free transportation included.

Itinerary

Day 1 — Arrival in Ngurah Rai International Airport, Kuta

Arrival at Ngurah Rai International Airport. Transfer and check-in at Grand Istana Rama Hotel (Kuta) or Amaris Hotel Seminyak (or similar 3-star hotel). Evening stroll at Kuta Beach and welcome dinner at a local warung.


Day 2 — Uluwatu Temple & Kecak Fire Dance

Visit Uluwatu Temple and enjoy panoramic ocean views. Watch the Kecak Fire Dance at sunset, followed by dinner at Jimbaran Bay.


Day 3 — Tanah Lot Temple & Seminyak

Explore Tanah Lot Temple and nearby shops. Afternoon free time in Seminyak for shopping or optional surf lesson at Kuta Beach.


Day 4 — Tegallalang Rice Terraces & Ubud

Visit Tegallalang Rice Terraces with optional Bali Swing. Explore Ubud Art Market and enjoy a scenic lunch overlooking the jungle.


Day 5 — Departure

Breakfast at the hotel, free time for souvenir shopping in Kuta, then transfer to Ngurah Rai International Airport for departure.
